The IUCN Red List of Threatened Species(tm) is widely recognized as the most comprehensive information source on the global status of animal, fungus and plant species. The IUCN Red List is based on an objective system for assessing the risk of extinction of a species. There are eight IUCN Red List Categories based on criteria linked to population size, trend and structure, and to geographic range. Species listed as Critically Endangered, Endangered and Vulnerable are collectively described as 'Threatened'.
